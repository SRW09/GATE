⚠️ Your Weak Areas (Priority Attack Zones)
🌡️ Thermodynamics (2nd Law & Cycles)
Otto, Diesel, Brayton, Rankine cycles = 5–10 marks consistently. Entropy & 2nd law are confusing traps. You flagged this. Expected to bounce back to 8+ marks in GATE 2027 after a dip in 2025.
→ Start with 1st Law, then build to cycles. P-V and T-S diagrams are essential visual tools.
🔧 Strength of Materials (Mohr's Circle & Deflection)
SOM = 8–15 marks. Mohr's circle, principal stresses, SFD-BMD, beam deflection asked in EVERY GATE exam 2007–2025 without exception.
→ Practice 10 Mohr's circle problems from scratch. Learn graphical method, not just formulas.
🌊 Heat Transfer
Spiked to 10 marks in GATE 2024! Fins, radiation view factors, and NTU heat exchanger methods are the biggest scoring areas. Likely to stay at 7–9 marks in 2027.
→ Master: 1D conduction, fin efficiency, LMTD/NTU, radiation (Fᵢⱼ summation rule always first).
⚙️ Theory of Machines & Vibrations
TOM has shifted heavily toward SDOF vibrations (natural frequency, damped frequency, logarithmic decrement) and epicyclic gear trains. 5–8 marks consistently.
→ Master the SDOF complete. For epicyclic gears: always use the tabular method — fastest and error-proof.
